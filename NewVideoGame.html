01 — Unity / 2D Platformer

Saguaro

A 2D platformer game developed in Unity with the Aztec Game Lab. The game is about a kangaroo rat traversing the Saguaro desert as it collects resources such as cactus spikes and seeds to defeat the snake boss. The project was completed with a group of 40 other people and is available on itch.io. I was a level designer for the project and was in charge of sketching out the main level as well as determining the player values such as jump height and speed.

02 — Unity / Top-Down

The Heist

A 2D top down game created with Unity in a team of 4 people. The game allows for players to traverse an abandoned bank taken over by robots as they steal cash and try to avoid getting caught by any robots. I contributed as programmer, level designer, art, and story for this project.

03 — Unity / Exploration

The Submarine

A 2D game created with a team of 6 people. The game allows players to explore a deep lake and meet large aquatic monsters. Players should be able to traverse and shoot at under sea boulders and collect resources. I acted as a programmer for this project.

04 — Unity / Puzzle

Cup Matching

Inspired by the Cup matching game from Tik Tok, this 2D Unity game allows users to drag and drop their guesses for a specific cup. Once the player has correctly identified all the cups positions, they will win the game.
